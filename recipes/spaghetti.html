Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This dish is adored by Nonnos and Nonnas everywhere. The quintessential Italian dish - pasta, beef and bolognese sauce!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaves
  • Beef cube or powder
  • Onion & garlic
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Red Wine
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti - 7 mins
  2. Saute garlic and onion in pan - 3 minutes
  3. Brown the beef in pan (optional: add red wine)
  4. Add all the other ingredients in pan
  5. Stir the pan, simmer - 20 minutes
  6. Dish up pasta, serve bolognese sauce over pasta
