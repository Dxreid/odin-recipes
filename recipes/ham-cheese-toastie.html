Ham & Cheese Toasted Sandwich

Description

This dish is a classic. Simple, easy to make & most importantly - Delicious! Sink your teeth into trio of ham, cheese & bread

Ingredients

  • Pre-sliced ham
  • Bread (White, wholemeal - whatever floats your boat!)
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter two bread slices
  2. Place cheese ontop of bread slice
  3. Place ham on top of cheese
  4. Place second breadslice on top of cheese
  5. Put the completed sandwich into sandwich press
  6. Dance!
  7. Remove completed toasted sandwich and put onto the plate
  8. Repeat above steps however many times necessary
