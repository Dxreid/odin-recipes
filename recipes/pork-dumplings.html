Pork & Chive Dumplings

Description

This is my favourite Sunday lunch dish to make with my wife. Classic Chinese dumplings with some Pork & Chive Fillings!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flower
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup chies
  • 500G ground pork
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Rice wine
  • 3 dashes white pepper powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Chinese black vinegar

Steps

  1. Mix flour and kneed - 15 minutes
  2. Separate dough into equal portions, roll into cylinders
  3. Cover with wet towel and set aside
  4. Chop the chives, mix with the ground pork - add in all ingredients
  5. Cut dough into 1/4 length, use rolling pin to flatten to round shape
  6. Put small filling in centre of the skin and seal tightly with your fingers
  7. Repeat above step to fill until all ingredients used
  8. Bring water to boil
  9. Put dumplings in boiling water and cover
  10. Once dumplings are floating, this means they're cooked
  11. Dish out dumplings with strainer, onto a serving platter
  12. Serve hot with black vinegar
